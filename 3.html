Building Bridges: A Peace Corps Classroom Guide to Cross-Cultural Understanding

The booklet provides short, adaptable lesson plans and activities that build cross-cultural awareness, respect, and communication in your classroom. Lessons are flexible, easy to use, and standards-based. The introduction explains more about the purpose of the book, its audience (grades 6 to 12), the terms used, the standards addressed, and more.

Building Bridges was supported in part by the National Geographic Society Education Foundation.
